## What the AGPL means for you

- ✅ **Free for everyone.** Use, copy, modify and redistribute the code at no cost.
- ✅ **Your music is yours.** Whatever you create and export belongs to you — the license covers the software, not your art.
- ✅ **Run it however you want.** Personal use, classroom, studio, live performance, research, open-source project — all fine.
- ⚠️ **If you redistribute or host a modified fork**, you must publish your modified source code under the AGPL-3.0 and make it available to your users. That's how AGPL keeps the commons growing.

This license is deliberate: every improvement someone makes to Xpendia Studio flows back to the community. Strudel and superdough — the audio engine Xpendia Studio is built on — are also AGPL-3.0. We respect their contract and inherit it.
